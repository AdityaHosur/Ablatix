Hate speech is not allowed, including attacks against individuals or groups based on race, religion, gender, or identity. Content promoting discrimination or exclusion is prohibited.

Violence or threats of violence are strictly prohibited, including encouraging harm, physical attacks, or dangerous acts.

Harassment, bullying, and abusive behavior targeting individuals or groups are not allowed.

Spam, scams, and deceptive or misleading content, including fraud and phishing, are prohibited.

Impersonation of individuals, brands, or organizations with intent to mislead is not allowed.

Sharing private or personal information without consent, including doxxing, is restricted.

Content promoting illegal activities, harmful behavior, or dangerous challenges is not allowed.
